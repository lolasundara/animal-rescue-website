Dave's Volunteer Experience in Kenya - Two weeks in Africa

Taking this volunteer trip to the Wildlife Sanctuary project was something I had wanted to do for several years. Unfortunately, between work and a busy schedule, it was always something that I “might” do “eventually” rather than actually doing. Then, I decided it had to happen. I made the preparations, booked my trip, and slightly hesitantly headed off to the airport. Unbeknownst to me it would be one of the best decisions I’ve ever made.
